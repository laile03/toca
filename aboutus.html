About Us

Our Mission

Toca is a community united for the love and passion for soccer. A project based on the intention of promoting and strengthening social relationships and networks.Many people play team sports at school and college but stop playing as they get older. We are all so busy that it's hard to organize a game, and we end up prioritising individual sports. The aim of Just Play is to change that. Playing great team sports are some of the best experience in life and we want to make it as easy as possible to play.

Teammates that become friends, friends that become family, a family that makes you feel like home.
